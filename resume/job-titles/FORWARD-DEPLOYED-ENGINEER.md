## What this role is

A **Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE)** — Palantir’s FDSE model, now adopted widely by AI companies (Anthropic, OpenAI, cloud vendors, enterprise SaaS) — **embeds with the customer** and owns the last mile: discovery → build → deploy → iterate in **their** environment, on **their** data, under **their** constraints (air-gapped, compliance, non-technical users, legacy systems).

FDEs write **production code**, not slide decks. Success = the customer runs the system without you. In 2026, FDE postings increasingly include **LLM pipeline configuration, fine-tuning support, and agent oversight** in messy enterprise settings.

Overlaps with **AI Engineer** (ML/LLM systems), **Data Scientist** (custom analytics for stakeholders), and **Public Technologist** (government/advocacy embeds) — same ACLU deployment story fits all three with different framing.

**Distinct emphasis vs siblings:** **customer proximity, autonomy, and shipped outcomes on client infrastructure** over algorithm lists or pure research.

## What employers look for (2025–2026 market)

| Priority | Signals |
|----------|---------|
| **Customer embed** | Requirements gathering, translating ops workflows, presenting to officials |
| **Full-stack delivery** | Prototype → production on client or private infra |
| **Data + ML at scale** | Pipelines, unstructured media, batch inference, integrations |
| **Autonomy & ambiguity** | Unblock without playbooks; scrappy CTO energy |
| **AI integration (rising)** | Configure LLM workflows, deploy models, operationalize agents |

**Typical stack:** Python, TypeScript/React, SQL, Docker, FastAPI, private/on-prem or regulated cloud, sometimes Spark.
