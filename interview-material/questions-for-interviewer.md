# Questions for Interviewer

## "What does the first 90 days look like for someone in this role — are you thrown into a customer engagement immediately or is there a ramp period?"

Shows you're thinking concretely about starting, not just getting the offer.

## "What kinds of companies are you deploying with right now — what industries or problem types are showing up most?"

Shows curiosity about the actual work, helps you tailor later answers.

## "What made the best forward deployed hire you've seen successful — what did they do in the first few months that others didn't?"

Better version of your good vs. great question, more specific.

## "How does the feedback loop from the field actually work — when an FDE finds a product gap, how does that make it back into the roadmap"

Shows you understand and care about the core mechanic of the role.

## "You mentioned talent density in the job description — what does that actually look like day to day? How do people push each other here?"

Shows you read carefully and want a real answer, not a PR answer.
