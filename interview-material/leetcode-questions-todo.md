## General Recommendations

Not Leetcode — but equally important for an MLE role.

- [ ] **StatQuest with Josh Starmer** — YouTube. Best visual ML explainers that exist. Watch the full ML fundamentals series.
- [ ] **Andrej Karpathy "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero"** — builds everything from scratch in Python. Transforms your intuition on how models actually work.
- [x] **Chip Huyen — "Designing Machine Learning Systems"** — the closest thing to a production ML textbook. Read chapters 1, 3, 4, and 7 at minimum.
- [ ] **Aurélien Géron — "Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow"** — best practical ML reference. Skim, don't read cover to cover.
- [ ] **MLflow quickstart** — instrument your quiz or auditor pipeline with MLflow tracking. One afternoon project, immediately resume-worthy.
